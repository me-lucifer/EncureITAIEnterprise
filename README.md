## üéØ The Opportunity

AI is transitioning from "experimentation phase" to "enterprise production phase" (2026-2027). Companies deploying AI at scale need **governance and security expertise** to:
- Prevent AI from being hacked (prompt injection, jailbreaks)
- Ensure AI accuracy (stop hallucinations)
- Comply with regulations (EU AI Act, August 2026)
- Protect sensitive data from leakage

**Market Size:** $15.8 billion by 2030 (30% CAGR)  
**Our Niche:** Mid-market companies (50-500 employees) underserved by Big 4 consultancies
