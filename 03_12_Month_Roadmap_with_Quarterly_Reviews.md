## Quarterly Review Process

### Review Meeting Structure (Last week of each quarter)

**Attendees:**
- CEO
- Practice Lead (AI Security Lead)
- Partnership Manager
- Product Manager
- Finance representative

**Agenda (2-hour meeting):**
1. **Metrics Review (30 min):** Review all success metrics vs targets
2. **Wins & Challenges (30 min):** What went well, what didn't
3. **Client Feedback (15 min):** Themes from client interactions
4. **Financial Review (15 min):** Revenue, costs, profitability
5. **Team Health (15 min):** Capacity, morale, skill gaps
6. **Next Quarter Planning (15 min):** Adjustments to roadmap

**Outputs:**
- Written review document
- Updated targets for next quarter
- Go/No-Go decision on major initiatives
- Action items with owners and deadlines

## Risk Management & Contingency Plans

### Risk: Slow Client Acquisition

**Early Warning Signs:**
- Less than 2 clients by end of Q2
- Conversion rate below 10% from proposals

**Contingency Plan:**
- Offer deeper discounts (up to 50% off) for first 5 clients
- Pivot to different target market (e.g., startups vs enterprises)
- Increase marketing spend
- Consider hiring dedicated sales person

---

### Risk: Service Delivery Issues

**Early Warning Signs:**
- Client complaints or low satisfaction scores
- Missed deadlines
- Team burnout

**Contingency Plan:**
- Pause new client acquisition
- Focus on fixing delivery processes
- Hire additional delivery staff
- Reduce scope of services temporarily

---

### Risk: Partnership Not Delivering

**Early Warning Signs:**
- No partner-sourced leads by Q3
- Tier advancement rejected
- Partner program changes unfavorably

**Contingency Plan:**
- Shift focus to other partners
- Invest more in independent (non-partner) services
- Build direct sales capabilities
- Consider different partnership models (e.g., technology alliances vs reseller)

---

### Risk: Competitive Pressure

**Early Warning Signs:**
- Losing deals to competitors
- Price pressure
- Competitors launching similar services

**Contingency Plan:**
- Accelerate proprietary tool development (differentiation)
- Focus on niche specialization (e.g., specific industry)
- Bundle services for better value proposition
- Emphasize our unique expertise and case studies
