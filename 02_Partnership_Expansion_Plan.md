## 1. Partnership Strategy Overview

**Goal:** Build a "Multi-Vendor Partnership Portfolio" to maximize market coverage and avoid single-vendor dependency.

**Why Multiple Partners?**
- **Market Coverage:** Different enterprises prefer different vendors (Microsoft shops vs IBM shops vs AWS shops)
- **Risk Mitigation:** Don't depend on one vendor's partner program changes
- **Competitive Advantage:** "Vendor-agnostic" positioning attracts clients tired of vendor lock-in
- **Revenue Diversification:** Multiple commission streams

## 7. Risk Mitigation

### Vendor Dependency Risk
- **Mitigation:** Multi-vendor strategy ensures no single vendor represents >40% of revenue
- **Monitoring:** Quarterly review of revenue mix by partner

### Program Changes Risk
- **Mitigation:** Stay engaged with partner advisory councils, early warning of program changes
- **Flexibility:** Build transferable skills (e.g., governance frameworks apply across vendors)

### Competitive Pressure Risk
- **Mitigation:** Specialize in mid-market (where big SIs don't focus)
- **Differentiation:** Build proprietary IP (our tools/dashboards) that complement vendor platforms
